    Slate Editor Hints

    Single-page form

    Build it in Slate

    Add fields in the form builder — they inherit kit styling via the mapping layer. Reserve the snippets here for the masthead, section accents, and "more info" toggles.

    Birthdate: use a single Date field, not the 3-dropdown Birthdate widget — the 3-select version emits empty labels (24 WAVE errors on your current form).

    Address: the Street Address field’s sub-parts each need a label. Confirm City/State/ZIP are labeled in the rendered output.

    Mark fields required in the builder; the kit adds the crimson left edge + asterisk automatically.

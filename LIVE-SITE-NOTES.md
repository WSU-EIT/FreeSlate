## admission.wsu.edu home — structure, voice, assets

**Hero:** H1 "Become a Coug" over Vimeo bg video (player.vimeo.com/video/773849648).
Voice: journey/trail metaphors, second person, "Take your first step…".
**Section H2 pattern:** plain + bold-second-word: "Start your **student journey**",
"Step into your **future**", "The Coug **Experience**", "**CONNECT** WITH WSU".

**Stat callouts (real, current):**
- "200+ undergraduate & graduate fields of study"
- "$403+ million in scholarships and grants"
- 400+ academic/cultural clubs · founded 1890 · community/school-spirit framing

**CTA labels in the wild:** Visit · Apply · Request Info · Find Out More ·
Apply Now · Apply Here · See what we offer · Check Your Eligibility ·
Visit or Tour Campus · Learn More About the Community

**Student types (the home page defines 7 — gold for form help text; write our
own tight versions, cite the page):** First-Year (applying for the year right
after high school, or no college since HS graduation) · Transfer (attended
college after HS, excluding the summer right after) · Graduate (has bachelor's,
pursuing Master's/Doctorate → gradschool.wsu.edu) · International (needs a visa
/ on a visa; not citizen, Green Card, DACA, asylee, refugee → ip.wsu.edu) ·
Non-Degree (coursework w/o degree intent: enrichment, transfer, teaching
endorsement) · Post-Bachelor's (bachelor's from a school other than WSU) ·
Returning (previously degree-seeking at any WSU campus, away >1 semester
excluding summer).

**Application portals (Slate!):** futurecoug.wsu.edu/portal/apply is THE apply
portal for first-year + transfer. Grad → gradschool.wsu.edu/apply. Intl →
ip.wsu.edu/future-students.

**Counselor finder controls:** campus select = Pullman / Spokane / Tri-Cities /

## Round 2 — request-information · first-year-students · dates-deadlines

**Request Information page:** breadcrumb (Admissions › Connect › Request Information),
H1 = breadcrumb leaf, Slate form arrives via embed_code (shows "Loading…"), then the
canonical Contact Us block + counselor finder. Confirms Template B anatomy for form pages.

**First-Year Students (voice + content gold):**
- H2 sequence: Welcome to WSU → Start Your Application → How to Apply →
  Explore degree offerings → Important Dates → Application Requirements →
  College Credit Earned in High School → Contact Us (last, always).
- How to Apply = 5 numbered steps; $70 application fee or fee waiver;
  priority date March 31 (first-year), Top Scholars by Jan 31; asterisk-footnote
  pattern with *italic* policy notes.
- "WSU does not require students to submit SAT/ACT test scores" — test-optional statement.
- CADR = WA College Academic Distribution Requirements (cadr.wsu.edu).
- First-year categories: HS Graduate / Students age 25+ (resume last 5 yrs +
  one-page essay via FutureCoug Portal doc uploader) / Homeschool (academic resume
  w/ parent signature or GED).
- Transcript rules: upload w/ app, FutureCoug Portal uploader, Parchment /
  National Student Clearinghouse / Scribbles / eScrip-Safe, mail, or fax —
  "We do not accept transcripts via email." Final transcripts direct from school.
- Disclaimer pattern: "This is a guide to the admission process, not a statement
  of policy" → link WSU General Catalog.
- CTAs: Find Your Admission Counselor (rep.wsu.edu) · Apply Now · Add WSU to
  Your Common App · Check Application Status (futurecoug.wsu.edu/portal/status).
- Images: wp-admission/uploads/sites/3144/2026/02/2025-NCO-Session-4-dh-_-710blur.jpg (hero),
  2026/02/Fall-Color_5275-1.jpg, 2026/02/2024FirstDayFall_4193-792x528.jpg.

Future Cougar Day

Experience Pullman for a day — campus tours, faculty meet-and-greets, student-life showcases, and a full taste of Coug culture.

About this event

Future Cougar Day gives prospective students an immersive look at what life at WSU Pullman really looks like. You'll join current Cougs for walking tours, sit in on a class, hear from financial aid advisors, and share a meal in one of our dining halls.

Best of all, it's completely free to attend — including lunch and parking.

The day's schedule

  • 8:30 a.m. — Check-in at the Lighty Student Services Building
  • 9:00 a.m. — Welcome session and campus overview
  • 10:00 a.m. — Walking campus tour (approx. 90 minutes)
  • 12:00 p.m. — Lunch in the residence dining halls
  • 1:00 p.m. — Academic department open houses by interest area
  • 2:30 p.m. — Financial aid and scholarship information session
  • 3:30 p.m. — Q&A with admissions counselors
  • 4:00 p.m. — Event concludes

Getting to Pullman

Pullman is served by Alaska Airlines from Seattle (SEA) with multiple daily flights. Driving from Seattle takes approximately 5 hours. Free visitor parking is available in Lot 16 on Stadium Way. Campus map →
